Shane Duff Realty: A Site to Call "Home"

Shane Duff is relatively new to real estate. He's been an agent for about a year, and until now he's borrowed a page on a fellow agent's website to inform visitors about him and generate leads. Whenever Shane received a real estate lead from his coworker's website, a percentage of the pay would have to go his fellow agent. After some time getting more comfortable in real estate, Shane decided he wanted to get started making his own website.

Shane wanted a simple site that could get potential property buyers and sellers connected with him. I created a baseline website in WordPress for him to get started, complete with contact form, testimonials, and links to his online listings. I look forward to continuing development on his site.
