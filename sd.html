Shane Duff Realty: A Site to Call "Home"

Shane Duff has been a real estate agent for a couple of years, and until now he's borrowed a page on a fellow agent's website to inform visitors about him and generate leads. Whenever Shane received a real estate lead from his coworker's website, a percentage of the pay would have to go his fellow agent. Shane decided he wanted to get started making his own website to get his own leads.

I worked with Shane to build a simple site to call his own. I created a baseline website in WordPress for us to get started, complete with contact form, testimonials, and links to his online listings. I look forward to continuing development on his site.
