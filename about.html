This portfolio was made from scratch using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
When playing drums in a garage band out of high school didn't bring me loads of money and fame, I decided it was time that I found another creative outlet that I could one day turn into a career.

Like a lot of other web designers in my age range, my first introduction to HTML was embedding YouTube videos into my MySpace page. Having a MySpace page at the age of eleven probably wasn't best for my exposure, but I did love the creative aspect of manipulating the webpage to express myself.
Most of my website projects up to this point have been fairly grassroots. I've been fortunate to work with clients based right here in my own city running small businesses.

In my time doing freelance work, I've become proficient with using:

• HTML and CSS

• Figma

• CMS platforms such as Wordpress

• and website builders such as Wix, SquareSpace, and Wordpress.
