Central Vacs Unlimited: Making a Site About Vacuums Less "Sucky"

Before working with me, Shane at Central Vacs Unlimited Inc. had a website that was very much... a website. It was a white background with a JPEG of his company logo, some contact information, and not much else. Shane feared that his site’s aging appearance would give visitors the impression that his business wasn’t even active. This is where I came in to help bring some life to his business website.
I created this concept in Figma first. Shane's customer base is older, so we wanted to keep the site simple and easy to navigate.
Older customers also rely heavily on reviews from other customers before doing business. Shane has a 5-star rating on Google, so we wajnted to make sure some of those outstanding reviews were on full display.
